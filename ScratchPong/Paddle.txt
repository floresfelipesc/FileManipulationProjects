WHATS:
What is the paddle?
The paddle is a thin rectangle that the player can control it's movement to move it in 2 directions 
up or down while staying within the range of the screen.
The paddle will be guarding the left wall of the screen.
The ball will bounce off the paddle

HOWS:
--How will the paddle be placed on screen?
It will start in the middle of the left wall of the screen

--How will the paddle move up and down?
Following the player's mouse y position only up to a defined limit depending on the screen. 



How will the paddle detect collision?
In another object
